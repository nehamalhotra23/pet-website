Tom is a puppy

Tom likes to do:

  • Eating a lot
  • Bitting Everything
  • Sleeping

He is a four legged animal which was originally bred from wolves. He make sounds such as bark, woof or arf. He has the ability to hear, smell and identify things very well but he have poor sight or to be specific, colour blindness, because of which they cannot see well in colour but can see better in dim light compared to humans. He eat both meat and vegetarian food.

Thanks for reading about Tom.
